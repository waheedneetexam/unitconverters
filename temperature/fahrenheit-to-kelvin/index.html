How to Convert Fahrenheit to Kelvin

1 Fahrenheit (°F) = 255.9277778 Kelvin (K)

1 Kelvin (K) = -457.87 Fahrenheit (°F)

Example: convert 20 Fahrenheit (°F) to Kelvin (K):
20 Fahrenheit (°F) = 266.4833333 Kelvin (K)

Fahrenheit Definition

Fahrenheit (°F) is a temperature scale where 32°F is the freezing point of water and 212°F is the boiling point. Used mainly in the United States.
