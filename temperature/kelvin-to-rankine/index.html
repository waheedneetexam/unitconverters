How to Convert Kelvin to Rankine

1 Kelvin (K) = 1.8 Rankine (°R)

1 Rankine (°R) = 0.5555555556 Kelvin (K)

Example: convert 20 Kelvin (K) to Rankine (°R):
20 Kelvin (K) = 36 Rankine (°R)

Kelvin Definition

Kelvin (K) is the SI base unit of temperature. 0 K is absolute zero, the lowest possible temperature.
