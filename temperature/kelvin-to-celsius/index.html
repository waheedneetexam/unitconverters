How to Convert Kelvin to Celsius

1 Kelvin (K) = -272.15 Celsius (°C)

1 Celsius (°C) = 274.15 Kelvin (K)

Example: convert 20 Kelvin (K) to Celsius (°C):
20 Kelvin (K) = -253.15 Celsius (°C)

Kelvin Definition

Kelvin (K) is the SI base unit of temperature. 0 K is absolute zero, the lowest possible temperature.
