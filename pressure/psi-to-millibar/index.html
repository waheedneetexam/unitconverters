How to Convert PSI to Millibar

1 PSI (psi) = 68.9476 Millibar (mbar)

1 Millibar (mbar) = 0.01450376808 PSI (psi)

Example: convert 15 PSI (psi) to Millibar (mbar):
15 PSI (psi) = 15 × 68.9476 Millibar (mbar) = 1,034.214 Millibar (mbar)

PSI Definition

PSI (pounds per square inch) equals 6,894.76 pascals. Used in the United States for tire and fluid pressure.
