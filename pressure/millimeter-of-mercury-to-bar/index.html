How to Convert Millimeter of Mercury to Bar

1 Millimeter of Mercury (mmHg) = 0.00133322 Bar (bar)

1 Bar (bar) = 750.0637554 Millimeter of Mercury (mmHg)

Example: convert 15 Millimeter of Mercury (mmHg) to Bar (bar):
15 Millimeter of Mercury (mmHg) = 15 × 0.00133322 Bar (bar) = 0.0199983 Bar (bar)

Millimeter of Mercury Definition

Millimeter of mercury (mmHg) equals 133.322 pascals. Used in medicine for blood pressure.
