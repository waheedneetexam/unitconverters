How to Convert Mach to Speed of Light

1 Mach (Ma) = 1.13508526e-06 Speed of Light (c)

1 Speed of Light (c) = 880,991 Mach (Ma)

Example: convert 15 Mach (Ma) to Speed of Light (c):

Mach Definition

Mach is the ratio of speed to the speed of sound. Mach 1 equals approximately 340.29 m/s at sea level.
