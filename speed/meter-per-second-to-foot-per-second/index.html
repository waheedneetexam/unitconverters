How to Convert Meter per Second to Foot per Second

1 Meter per Second (m/s) = 3.280839895 Foot per Second (ft/s)

1 Foot per Second (ft/s) = 0.3048 Meter per Second (m/s)

Example: convert 15 Meter per Second (m/s) to Foot per Second (ft/s):
15 Meter per Second (m/s) = 15 × 3.280839895 Foot per Second (ft/s) = 49.21259843 Foot per Second (ft/s)

Meter per Second Definition

Meters per second (m/s) is the SI unit of speed. It measures how many meters are traveled in one second.
