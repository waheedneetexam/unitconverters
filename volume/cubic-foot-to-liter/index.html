How to Convert Cubic Foot to Liter

1 Cubic Foot (ft³) = 28.3168 Liter (L)

1 Liter (L) = 0.03531472483 Cubic Foot (ft³)

Example: convert 15 Cubic Foot (ft³) to Liter (L):
15 Cubic Foot (ft³) = 15 × 28.3168 Liter (L) = 424.752 Liter (L)

Cubic Foot Definition

A cubic foot equals 28.3168 liters. Used in the United States for volume of rooms and containers.
