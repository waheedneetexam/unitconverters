How to Convert US Fluid Ounce to US Pint

1 US Fluid Ounce (fl oz) = 0.0625 US Pint (pt)

1 US Pint (pt) = 16 US Fluid Ounce (fl oz)

Example: convert 15 US Fluid Ounce (fl oz) to US Pint (pt):
15 US Fluid Ounce (fl oz) = 15 × 0.0625 US Pint (pt) = 0.9375 US Pint (pt)

US Fluid Ounce Definition

A US fluid ounce equals 0.0295735 liters. Used for small liquid measurements.
