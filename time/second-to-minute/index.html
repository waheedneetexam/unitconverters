How to Convert Second to Minute

1 Second (s) = 0.01666666667 Minute (min)

1 Minute (min) = 60 Second (s)

Example: convert 15 Second (s) to Minute (min):
15 Second (s) = 15 × 0.01666666667 Minute (min) = 0.25 Minute (min)

Second Definition

The second is the SI base unit of time. It is defined by the cesium-133 atomic clock.
