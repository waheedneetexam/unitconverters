How to Convert Stone to Gram

1 Stone (st) = 6,350.29 Gram (g)

1 Gram (g) = 0.0001574731233 Stone (st)

Example: convert 15 Stone (st) to Gram (g):
15 Stone (st) = 15 × 6,350.29 Gram (g) = 95,254.35 Gram (g)

Stone Definition

A stone equals 6.35029 kilograms or 14 pounds. Used in the United Kingdom for body weight.
