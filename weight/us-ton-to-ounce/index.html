How to Convert US Ton to Ounce

1 US Ton (ton) = 32,000 Ounce (oz)

1 Ounce (oz) = 3.124996555e-05 US Ton (ton)

Example: convert 15 US Ton (ton) to Ounce (oz):
15 US Ton (ton) = 15 × 32,000 Ounce (oz) = 480,001 Ounce (oz)

US Ton Definition

A US ton (short ton) equals 907.185 kilograms or 2,000 pounds.
