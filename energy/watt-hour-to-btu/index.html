How to Convert Watt-Hour to BTU

1 Watt-Hour (Wh) = 3.41212822 BTU (BTU)

1 BTU (BTU) = 0.2930722222 Watt-Hour (Wh)

Example: convert 15 Watt-Hour (Wh) to BTU (BTU):
15 Watt-Hour (Wh) = 15 × 3.41212822 BTU (BTU) = 51.1819233 BTU (BTU)

Watt-Hour Definition

A watt-hour equals 3,600 joules. Used for measuring electrical energy consumption.
