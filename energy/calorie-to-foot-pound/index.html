How to Convert Calorie to Foot-Pound

1 Calorie (cal) = 3.085955363 Foot-Pound (ft·lb)

1 Foot-Pound (ft·lb) = 0.3240487572 Calorie (cal)

Example: convert 15 Calorie (cal) to Foot-Pound (ft·lb):
15 Calorie (cal) = 15 × 3.085955363 Foot-Pound (ft·lb) = 46.28933044 Foot-Pound (ft·lb)

Calorie Definition

A calorie equals 4.184 joules. It is the energy needed to raise 1 gram of water by 1°C.
