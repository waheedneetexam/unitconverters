How to Convert Therm to Megawatt-Hour

1 Therm (thm) = 0.02930555556 Megawatt-Hour (MWh)

1 Megawatt-Hour (MWh) = 34.12322275 Therm (thm)

Example: convert 15 Therm (thm) to Megawatt-Hour (MWh):
15 Therm (thm) = 15 × 0.02930555556 Megawatt-Hour (MWh) = 0.4395833333 Megawatt-Hour (MWh)

Therm Definition

A therm equals 105,480,400 joules or 100,000 BTU. Used for natural gas billing.
