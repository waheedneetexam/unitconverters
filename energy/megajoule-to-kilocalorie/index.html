How to Convert Megajoule to Kilocalorie

1 Megajoule (MJ) = 239.0057361 Kilocalorie (kcal)

1 Kilocalorie (kcal) = 0.004184 Megajoule (MJ)

Example: convert 15 Megajoule (MJ) to Kilocalorie (kcal):
15 Megajoule (MJ) = 15 × 239.0057361 Kilocalorie (kcal) = 3,585.086042 Kilocalorie (kcal)

Megajoule Definition

A megajoule equals 1,000,000 joules. Used in engineering and physics.
