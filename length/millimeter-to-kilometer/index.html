Convert Millimeter to Kilometer

Kilometer (km)

How to Convert Millimeter to Kilometer

1 Millimeter (mm) = 0.000001 Kilometer (km)

1 Kilometer (km) = 1,000,000 Millimeter (mm)

Example: convert 15 Millimeter (mm) to Kilometer (km):
15 Millimeter (mm) = 15 × 0.000001 Kilometer (km) = 1.5e-05 Kilometer (km)

Millimeter Definition

A millimeter is one thousandth of a meter. It is used for precise measurements in engineering and manufacturing.
