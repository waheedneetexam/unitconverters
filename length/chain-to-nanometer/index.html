How to Convert Chain to Nanometer

1 Chain (ch) = 2.01168e+10 Nanometer (nm)

1 Nanometer (nm) = 4.970969538e-11 Chain (ch)

Example: convert 15 Chain (ch) to Nanometer (nm):
15 Chain (ch) = 15 × 2.01168e+10 Nanometer (nm) = 3.01752e+11 Nanometer (nm)

Chain Definition

A chain equals 20.1168 meters. It is a unit of length used in surveying.
