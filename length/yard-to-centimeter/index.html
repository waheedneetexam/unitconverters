How to Convert Yard to Centimeter

1 Yard (yd) = 91.44 Centimeter (cm)

1 Centimeter (cm) = 0.01093613298 Yard (yd)

Example: convert 15 Yard (yd) to Centimeter (cm):
15 Yard (yd) = 15 × 91.44 Centimeter (cm) = 1,371.6 Centimeter (cm)

Yard Definition

A yard equals 3 feet or 36 inches. It is used in the United States and United Kingdom for measuring fabric and sports fields.
