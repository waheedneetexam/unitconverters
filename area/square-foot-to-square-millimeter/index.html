How to Convert Square Foot to Square Millimeter

1 Square Foot (ft²) = 92,903 Square Millimeter (mm²)

1 Square Millimeter (mm²) = 1.076391505e-05 Square Foot (ft²)

Example: convert 15 Square Foot (ft²) to Square Millimeter (mm²):
15 Square Foot (ft²) = 15 × 92,903 Square Millimeter (mm²) = 1.39354e+06 Square Millimeter (mm²)
