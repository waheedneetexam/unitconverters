How to Convert Square Meter to Square Foot

1 Square Meter (m²) = 10.76391505 Square Foot (ft²)

1 Square Foot (ft²) = 0.092903 Square Meter (m²)

Example: convert 15 Square Meter (m²) to Square Foot (ft²):
15 Square Meter (m²) = 15 × 10.76391505 Square Foot (ft²) = 161.4587258 Square Foot (ft²)

Square Meter Definition

A square meter is the SI unit of area, equal to the area of a square with sides of one meter.
